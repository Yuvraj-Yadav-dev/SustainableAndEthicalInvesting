What is Sustainable Investing?

Sustainable investing focuses on investing in companies that prioritize environmental protection, social responsibility, and ethical governance practices. Investors evaluate not only financial performance but also how companies impact society and the environment.

Understanding ESG

🌱 Environmental

Focuses on how companies protect the environment through reducing emissions, renewable energy, and waste management.

🤝 Social

Examines how companies treat employees, customers, and communities.

🏛️ Governance

Focuses on company leadership, transparency, and ethical business practices.

Glossary

  • ESG – Environmental, Social, Governance investing criteria
  • SRI – Socially Responsible Investing
  • Impact Investing – Investments generating social impact
  • Carbon Footprint – Amount of carbon emissions produced
  • Green Bonds – Bonds used to fund environmental projects
